THE PINK CITY

Jaipur, also known as the pink city, is one of the most beautiful and culturally rich cities in India.
It was founded by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II in November 1727 and also got its name from this brave Hindu ruler.
Located in Northern India, Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and also the largest city in this state.
